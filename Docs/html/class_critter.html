void Critter.ApplyDamage ( float  damage,
float  slowMod 
)

Applies damage to the critter by deducting a damage value from its HealthPoints.

The critter is killed if its HealthPoints hits or falls below 0.

The slowMod is applied if that is the case.

Sets the difficulty and updates the health and reward cash attributes of this critter accordingly.

HealthPoints is increased by (diffucltyIncrease * 100)% and the reward cash, Cash, is increased by itself * difficultyIncrease * 2.
